Thinking About You

THINKING ABOUT YOU is the debut album from SHDE, released on June 10th, 2022. The album delves into the complexities of falling in love while grappling with the fear of getting hurt. It narrates the journey of a 15-year-old SHDE as he searches for "the one," navigating the intense emotions of love, lust, jealousy, and heartbreak.

Comprising 9 tracks, the album blends elements of pop, trap, and emo rap, creating a sound reminiscent of artists like Juice WRLD, The Kid LAROI, and Iann Dior.
